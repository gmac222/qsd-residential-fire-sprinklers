Our Mission

Following the Building Safety Act 2022, compliance has never been more critical. Our mission is to remove the complexity of fire safety compliance for residential property managers and developers. We provide an end-to-end service—from initial design and hydraulic calculation to installation, commissioning, and ongoing maintenance.

Unrivalled Expertise

Our workforce consists of dedicated, IFC-certified engineers possessing unrivalled expertise in passive and active fire protection. Based near Wrexham, North Wales, our strategic headquarters allows us to effectively deploy our teams to projects nationwide.

  • Design & Specification: Tailored CAD designs ensuring optimal coverage with minimal aesthetic disruption.
  • Installation: Swift, professional installation by highly trained BAFSA-accredited teams.
  • Maintenance & Servicing: Comprehensive planned preventative maintenance (PPM) to guarantee ongoing compliance and operational readiness.
