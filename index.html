Our Process

End-to-End Compliance Delivery

We handle everything from the initial water pressure test to the final handover certificate, ensuring a seamless experience for your accountable person.

Survey & Design

We conduct a free site survey, verify mains pressure, and produce fully hydraulically calculated designs to BS 9251:2021 standards.

Installation

Our IFC-certified engineers install your system safely. For retrofits, we employ strict resident-liaison protocols to ensure minimal disruption.

Commissioning

Systems are comprehensively pressure-tested and commissioned, providing you with all necessary handover documentation for your Golden Thread.

Maintenance

We offer fixed-fee annual inspection and maintenance contracts to ensure ongoing regulatory compliance year after year.

What we deliver
